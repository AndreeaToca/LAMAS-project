The riddle, called the 23-logicians was proposed by Edwin Meyer (TED-Ed source), and resemblance other classic, epistemic logic puzzles such as Muddy Children problem in Meyerand van der Hoek.

Our Riddle

Each year, thousands of logicians meet up in the desert for Learning Man, a summer festival where attendees solve problems together, as well as party. At the last night of the festival, an exclusive party in the middle -- the Logician's Rave -- takes place. To prove they are worthy of entering, anyone who wishes to join must get past the Demon of Reason: a friendly creature, yet infamous for its challenging puzzles it gives to anyone wishing to pass. Only those who manage to solve the demon's puzzle are allowed to enter. You are visiting Learning Man, and ask the demon to let you enter; before it lets you do so, he poses you the following puzzle:
23 logician friends of you have already entered the Rave before you. Before the demon let them in, it placed hats on each of your friends' heads, and placed the friends in a circle, so they can face each other, without seeing their own hat. The demon then explains:
  1. Each of you wears a hat of precisely one color

  2. Each of you can see the color of the hats of your friends, but you may not -- and cannot, by a spell of mine -- see that of your own.

  3. You may not communicate with the others.

  4. Once every minute I ask whether anyone that has figured out their hats' color to step forward and announce it to me; If they are correct, I will give them entry to the Rave. If they know their color, they must step up immediately, and not wait for a later minute.

  5. I selected the hats in such a way, that each of you will be able to figure out your hats' color at some point.''

Knowing your friends well, you are also confident that your 23 logician friends play the game perfectly. The demon proceeds: Over the course of four minutes, the following happened:
  • After the first minute elapsed, four friends deduced their hat's color.
  • A minute later, multiple other logicians found out their color: red
  • Once minute three passed, nobody new could deduce their mask's color.
  • At the fourth minute, logicians in two colored hats correctly announced their hat's color.''
Now the demon asks you: How many logicians entered the Rave at the fifth minute?
